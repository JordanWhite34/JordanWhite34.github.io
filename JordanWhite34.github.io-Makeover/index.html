This allowed me to efficiently manage and scale the game's resources as needed, providing a consistent and enjoyable gaming experience for players.

A significant aspect of the game's success is its reliance on the state-of-the-art NLP engine, Perplexity. Additionally, I wrote regression tests for the engine, ensuring its reliability and performance.

To further enhance the game's NLP capabilities, I utilized Cyberduck to collect user data, which enabled me to analyze player interactions and refine the game's understanding of natural language commands. This iterative process of improvement allowed me to deliver a more immersive and intuitive gaming experience that adapts to individual players' language preferences and styles.

In summary, my advanced text-based video game showcases my expertise in natural language processing, programming, and cloud-based infrastructure management. As a result, I have demonstrated my ability to create compelling and technologically advanced products that cater to users' needs and preferences. My commitment to continuous improvement, adaptability, and understanding of cutting-edge technologies make me a valuable candidate for any team seeking a talented software engineer or machine learning expert.

PCA, Clustering

In this project, I successfully implemented and applied advanced machine learning techniques such as Principal Component Analysis (PCA) and Clustering (k-Means and Gaussian Mixture Model) to analyze and process high-dimensional data. This experience has significantly contributed to my growth as a data scientist, and allowed me to develop and showcase my proficiency in using Python for machine learning tasks.

Throughout the project, I demonstrated a deep understanding of the underlying mathematical concepts and computational methods involved in PCA and Clustering. I implemented PCA from scratch using the NumPy library, which enabled me to effectively reduce the dimensionality of the ORL Face Data dataset while preserving as much information as possible. This hands-on experience has enhanced my understanding of dimensionality reduction techniques and their applications in real-world scenarios.

In addition to PCA, I implemented the k-Means clustering algorithm from scratch and utilized the Gaussian Mixture Model (GMM) clustering algorithm from the scikit-learn package. I employed these clustering techniques to analyze and classify the well-known MNIST database of handwritten digits. This part of the project required me to make informed decisions on the number of clusters and the optimal dimensions for PCA reduction, which has further developed my critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Throughout the project, I showcased my strong Python programming skills and my ability to leverage popular libraries such as NumPy, pandas, matplotlib, and scikit-learn. I also demonstrated the importance of clear and effective data visualization when presenting complex results, which is essential for conveying meaningful insights to both technical and non-technical audiences.

Overall, this project has allowed me to further develop and demonstrate my expertise in machine learning, data analysis, and programming. I am confident that my experience with this project will serve as a strong foundation for future endeavors in the field of data science and machine learning.
